ABOUT US

OUR GOAL

Helping businesses deliver exceptional buyer experiences.

One Stop Arcade is a property management company with a focus on providing comprehensive solutions for retail properties. Our aim is to work closely with our clients to develop custom plans that are dedicated to their specific needs and in return offer an exceptional service by optimising the value of the property and increasing revenue.

Contact us

Our Services

We offer a wide range of property management to retail property owners including but not limited to property maintenance, lease administration, financial management, marketing and operations.

We work closely with our clients to design custom plans that are unique to their needs and ensure that their properties are well maintained, secure and fully leased.

Marketing

We create marketing strategies using social media, newsletters, billboards, and events to attract tenants.

Lease Administration

We handle tenant screening, lease negotiations, renewals, and provide accurate financial planning.

Financial Management

We provide financial reporting and maximize revenue through expense management and performance insight.
