Developing and managing Nairobi convenience properties.

One Stop Arcade Holdings (OSAH) develops and manages convenience retail properties in Nairobi, Kenya. Currently operating in Karen, the company is expanding with new developments in Nyari and Ruaka, Kiambu County.

Enter One Stop Arcade, best in retail

One Stop Arcade is planning on an aggressive expansion seeking to expand our best-in-class retail property portfolio to all major urban areas within Kenya and thereafter in the East African region. Our main offices are located at Zamani Office Park, Karen â€“ Lower-Level Suite B-2.

Our Businesses

Our business rests on four pillars, supporting diverse operations, enhancing experiences, fostering community engagement, and driving sustainable growth across all ventures.

PROPERTY DEVELOPMENT

As developers, we specialize in creating convenience shopping centers owned and operated wholly by One Stop Arcade Holdings. In this category wholly owned OSAH subsidiaries develop and manage all aspects of the development and ultimately operate the completed properties under our trading brand One Stop Arcade. It is envisioned that all buildings developed under this model will also operate under the One Stop Arcade brand.

PROPERTY INVESTMENT

OSAH is also an investor seeking to collaborate with developers in the retail sector, serving either as active or silent investors, where we seek to leverage our knowledge and intelligence within this sub-sector to further grow our business. In this category, the properties may or may not operate under the One Stop Arcade brand and shareholding will differ from a small to significant minority shareholding.

PROPERTY MANAGEMENT

OSAH also seeks to further leverage our retail property management experience with other developers in the retail space by offering project management services. OSAH may have no shareholding but offers project management services including handling pre-construction, on-construction, and post-construction services.

OSAH may also be engaged for the day-to-day running of these properties through its affiliated management company One Stop Arcade Management Ltd. Services offered include cleaning, security, maintenance, rent collection, financial accounting, and compliance with all government regulations.

PROPERTY LETTING

OSAH seeks to connect property owners and managers with retailers, assisting them to secure suitable retail spaces. Our dedicated marketing team is always on the lookout for information on available properties or properties under development which not only informs our development decisions but also provides avenues for advising our retail partners on available locations in the marketplace.

One Stop Arcade Langata

Completed: July 2023

Completed in July 2023, One Stop Arcade - Langata Road is a bustling three-story, 50,000 sf convenience mall located on a busy artery also within the Karen area of Nairobi.
This vibrant shopping destination is known for its diverse array of stores with services and products catering to the needs of both locals and visitors.

Situated strategically at the corner of Langata Road and Langata Road South, the property enjoys a prime location with ease of access for all shoppers within the Karen-Langata catchment area and in-transit customers from areas as far as Ngong and Rongai.
It is a three-floor development with an impressive selection of retail outlets, restaurants, and essential services, making it a one-stop destination for shopping, dining, and leisure.

One Stop Arcade - Langata Road boasts Naivas Supermarket as its anchor tenant, further solidifying its position as a premier shopping destination in Karen, Nairobi. As a well-established supermarket chain known for its wide selection of groceries, fresh produce, household goods, and more, Naivas draws in a steady flow of shoppers seeking convenience and quality. Its presence enhances the overall appeal of the mall, offering visitors a comprehensive shopping experience under one roof. Whether patrons are stocking up on essentials or exploring other offerings within the mall, Naivas Supermarket serves as a key attraction, contributing to the vibrant atmosphere and success of One Stop Arcade - Langata Road.

With over 80% occupancy, One Stop Arcade - Langata Road buzzes with activity, drawing in crowds with its mix of well-known brands and local businesses. From fashion boutiques and electronics stores to grocery shops and beauty salons, the mall offers a comprehensive shopping experience tailored to diverse tastes and preferences.

Whether you're in need of everyday essentials, searching for the latest fashion trends, or simply looking to unwind over a delicious meal, One Stop Arcade - Langata Road provides a welcoming and convenient environment for all.

Its lively atmosphere, coupled with a commitment to customer satisfaction, makes it a beloved hub in the heart of Karen, Nairobi.
